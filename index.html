Tips

  • Arrow keys or A and D to move
  • Space to jump
  • Shift to sprint
  • Each coin you get gives you one jump. Use it wisely!
  • You can only store a maximum of 3 jump charges
  • Press enter to select the highlighted button
  • Press R to retry
